Financial Services

From traditional leasing programs to comprehensive rental models

Introduction

Financial Services refer to different options for customers to obtain a Company Owned Instrument. It allows customers with a low Capital Budget (CapEx) to have an Illumina instrument installed on site.
‍
Company Owned Instruments are installed products that are owned by Illumina and are placed on a customer’s site. It excludes instruments classed as a Service Loaner.

We make a distinction between financial and non-financial options. Non-financial options are our sales loaners and demos, which are not part of the Financial Services portfolio. The financial options are our Rental Systems, Reagent Rentals and Leasing options.

Instrument Lease

Financial solution to rent/lease equipment via external leasing partner.

A lease is a contract directly between the customer and the leasing company. Illumina sells the required instrument to the Leasing partner who then rents it out to the end customer. The instrument is shipped directly to the end customer from Illumina.

EMEA Leasing Partners :

EvoScience

Please create a Direct Standard – Instrument Lease opportunity in Salesforce with the naming convention:

"System type_ Lease_Account_Customername"

An instrument lease is a standard instrument sale to the leasing company. Therefore there is no record needed in the Quickbase app EMEA Capital Support Management System.

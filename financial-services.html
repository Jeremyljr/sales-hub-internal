Financial Services

From traditional leasing programs to comprehensive rental models

Introduction

Financial Services refer to different options for customers to obtain a Company Owned Instrument. It allows customers with a low Capital Budget (CapEx) to have an Illumina instrument installed on site.
‍
Company Owned Instruments are installed products that are owned by Illumina and are placed on a customer’s site. It excludes instruments classed as a Service Loaner.

We make a distinction between financial and non-financial options. Non-financial options are our sales loaners and demos, which are not part of the Financial Services portfolio. The financial options are our Rental Systems, Reagent Rentals and Leasing options.

vFast

Please go to the Virtual Financial Assistant (vFast) to model reagent rental proposals. For more information on how to use the tool, explore these tutorials or contact financialservices@illumina.com

For agreements higher than $1M, it is recommended to include your Finance Business Partner early in the process to advise you on the financial health of the deal.
Any proposal with a pre-discount value higher than $5M will need to go through the Big Deal Review process. In this case, the Finance Business Partner will help you to compile the Big Deal Review package, present it to top management and obtain the necessary approvals.

Quickbase

For each agreement, a record needs to be created in EMEA Capital Support Management System app. This enables visibility of all steps in the process and facilitates obtaining approvals. For more information on how to use the tool, explore this tutorial.

Editing access is granted to TAMs, Inside Sales and members of the departments Quotes, Contracts and Finance. Read Only access is granted to Sales Specialists.

Note: We only need 1 record in Quickbase per agreement!

Instrument Lease

Financial solution to rent/lease equipment via external leasing partner.

A lease is a contract directly between the customer and the leasing company. Illumina sells the required instrument to the Leasing partner who then rents it out to the end customer. The instrument is shipped directly to the end customer from Illumina.

Sales Loaner / Demo

Please create a Direct Standard – Standard Sale opportunity in salesforce in salesforce.
The naming convention is: System _ SalesLoaner_Account_Customer name

A sales loaner or sales demo will not require modelling a proposal. For a benchtop sales demo we do require to check if there are already any benchtop demo’s placed within the district, as we cannot place more then 3 in one district. Please contact financialservices@illumina.com to check this for you.

‍

We always use new instruments for sales loaners/demo’s since the objective of the loaner or demo is for the customer to buy it after the agreed period. You can check with Customer Care to make sure an instrument is available from stock.

IVDR: The Basics, Health Institution Exemption and Implementation Changes

Date: 3 February 2022 (2pm - 4pm GMT)

Facilitator: Jonathan Parker

Location: Virtual Class

In this training session you will learn in more detail about the upcoming IVDR in Europe (implementation from 26th May 2022). Gain a better understanding of the upcoming IVDR focusing particularly on the health institution definition and application of the exemption.

Understanding of this regulation is crucial to being able to support customers in their transition to IVDR compliance and guiding them through which Illumina products can be positioned and how.

This will allow all customer-facing roles, where IVDR is applicable, to confidently hold conversations about how Illumina can support in the run up to the IVDR introduction and beyond.

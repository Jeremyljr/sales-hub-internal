What are you looking for?

Actual Trend & Pipeline Detail

Gain quarterly trend insights, roll up and pipeline details at the customer and product level with the ability to multi filter.

Monitor library prep ratios by institution.

DeepDive

Gain top level and detailed information on all EMEA customers, districts and  territories, and related transactional data.

   View deltas and monitor changes across the board on the home page, or drill down in Table Extra with multi level filters.

Rollup Overview

Understand where the territory rollup is versus forecast and view additional details into what areas have contributed to the overall rollup.

Identify opportunities and view pipeline health by territory, broken down into SFDC Stages.

Order Line Details

View aggregated order information, including posted orders & shipments,  anticipated backlog ship schedules, and order hold information.

Proactively monitor order progression with order fulfillment schedule and order line status and associated detail information.

MBO Dashboard

Enable TAMs, PAMs and DSMs to monitor MBO metrics aligned to the company goals for the current year.

Follow quarterly progress against pre-agreed targets.

Pull Through

Review  pull through trends across territories, platform and customers to inform utilization growth strategy.
‍
Highlight under & over-performing customers and help target strategic consumable conversations.

Account 360

Deep dive into an existing account to gather the commercial data & insights to  drive business and improve the customer experience.

View everything from account history and installed products, to service issues and pull through to zoom in on a particular account.

New Service Contract Dashboard

Identify new contract opportunities and assess existing service contracts. Quickly analyze the coverage of installed assets by district or by product.

Focused on analyzing data for the customer who holds the service contract, unlike the CS dashboard who is organised by the customer who is the guardian of the asset.

Commercial 360

Offers key commercial business performance overview. Visually assess the customer mapping for UC customers with active assets.

Visually compare and assess the sales and service performance change over time.

Growth Overview

Understand the growth areas of the business and understand the impact of top/bottom accounts on overall company growth.

Whilst Commercial 360 offers a high-level view on business performance, Growth Overview allows to zoom in on the source of the growth.

Illumina Proactive Runs Dashboard

Global  and Regional proactive runs at a glance. Proactively monitor connectivity  patterns and view runs over time in order to identify outliers and provide  enhanced customer support.

Compare proactive runs vs baseline trend for a particular customer to ensure optimal performance.

Pipeline Health

Using different sales forecast versions, view how forecast compares to rollup  across instruments, consumables, and services/other.

Review how forecasts change over time and how well the existing pipeline is suited to achieve forecast goals.

NIPT Account 360 View

Summary data of NIPT materials sold year over year by UC Country.

View historical spend by Country, Product Category and Historical MicroArray.

Informatics

Proactively monitor contract status & renewal opportunities.

Review pipeline opportunities to identify areas for informatics.

Rollup Movement

Understand how your rollup has changed period over period.

Identify the opportunities & orders driving the increase or decrease in your rollup.

New Grants Dashboard

Gain insight into the different levels of funding for EMEA-based institutions,  listing everyone in relationship to the grant.

Better understand market and geographical research trends, identify categories with funding growth and  create new opportunities.

Discount Overview

Evaluate historical discounting to inform future pricing decisions. Understand how discounting compares across territories, customers, and products.

View information on a customer's standing quote and their shipments against that quote in the Standing Quote Performance tab.
